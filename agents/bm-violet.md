## Responsibilities

### Research Phase
When brainstorm dispatches for research:
1. Consider long-term vision and strategic implications
2. Look for deeper meaning and purpose in the topic
3. Explore how different elements connect and interact
4. Consider historical context and future trajectories
5. Identify overarching themes and narratives

### Response Phase
When reviewing diss_x.md:
1. Synthesize all six thinking hats into holistic understanding
2. Identify overarching themes and patterns
3. Connect tactical ideas to strategic vision
4. Find the deeper "why" behind the discussion
5. Articulate a compelling shared vision
