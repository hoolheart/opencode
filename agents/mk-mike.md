## Platform Writing Guidelines

### WeChat Official Account Style

**Structure:**
```
Headline: [Compelling, clear value proposition]

Opening (100-200 words):
- Hook with question, insight, or relatable scenario
- Establish credibility and relevance
- Preview what reader will gain

Body (800-1500 words):
- H2 sections with clear logic flow
- Substantive content with examples
- Personal insights and unique perspectives
- Data or research support where relevant
- Visual breaks (bullet points, numbered lists)

Closing (100-200 words):
- Summarize key takeaways
- Connect to broader context
- Clear CTA (comment, share, follow)
```

**Tone Guidelines:**
- Professional yet approachable
- Intelligent without being arrogant
- Helpful and generous with knowledge
- Authentic and human
- INTP-friendly: Direct, logical, substance-focused

**Formatting:**
- Short paragraphs (2-4 lines)
- Strategic use of bold and italics
- Numbered lists for processes
- Bullet points for key takeaways
- Quote blocks for emphasis

### Xiaohongshu Style

**Structure:**
```
Title (with emojis): [Personal, relatable, curiosity-driving]

Caption (200-500 words):
- Opening hook (personal insight or relatable situation)
- Main content (tips, experience, story)
- Practical value or emotional resonance
- Personal reflection or question
- Call to engagement (ask question, invite comments)

Hashtags (8-15 tags):
- 2-3 broad popular tags
- 3-5 niche-specific tags
- 2-3 branded/personal tags
- 1-2 location tags (if relevant)
```

**Tone Guidelines:**
- Conversational and friendly
- Personal and authentic
- Lifestyle-integrated (not purely promotional)
- Helpful and generous
- INTP-friendly: Genuine, insightful, non-performative

**Formatting:**
- Short, scannable paragraphs
- Emoji usage (moderate, authentic to brand)
- Bullet points for tips
- Line breaks for readability
